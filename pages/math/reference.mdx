# Reference
	
_This page serves as a reference for commonly used mathematical propositions._


### Exponents
> __~lemma~.__ Where ${x \in \reals,}$

$$
  \eqs{
    &x^1 = x \\
    &x^2 = x \by x \\
    &x^3 = x \by x \by x \\
    &x^4 = x \by x \by x \by x \\
    &\vdots \\
    &x^n = x_1 \by x_2 \by \ldots \by x_{n-1} \by x_{n} \\
  }
$$

<Grid cols={2}>

> __~lemma~.__ ${x^1 = x.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${x^0 = 1.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${x^{-1} = \dfrac{1}{x} \to x \neq 0.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${(x^m)(x^n) = x^{m+n}.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${\dfrac{x^m}{x^n} = x^{m-n} \to x^n \neq 0.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${(x^m)^n = x^{m \by n}.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${(xy)^n = (x^n)(y^n)}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${\ar{\dfrac{x}{y}}^n = \dfrac{x^n}{y^n} \to y^n \neq 0.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${x^{-n} = \dfrac{1}{x^n}.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${x^{m/n} = \sqrt[n]{x^m} = \ar{\sqrt[n]{x}}^m.}$

</Grid>

### Roots
<Grid cols={2}>

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${b \ge 0,}$ ${\sqrt{b} = b^2}$

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${b \ge 0,}$ ${\sqrt{b^n} = b}$

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${b \ge 0}$ and ${a \ge 0,}$ ${\sqrt{a \by b} = \sqrt{a} \by \sqrt{b}}$

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${b \gt 0}$ and ${a \ge 0,}$ ${\sqrt{\dfrac{a}{b}} = \dfrac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{b}}}$

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${b \ge 0}$ ${\ar{\sqrt{b}}\ar{\sqrt{b}} = b}$

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${b \ge 0,}$ ${\sqrt{b^x} = \ar{\sqrt{b}}^x = b^{x/n}.}$

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${a \gt 0,}$ ${b \gt 0,}$ and ${c \gt 0,}$
> 
> $$
> \eqs{
>    \dfrac{a}{\sqrt{b}}
>    &= \dfrac{a}{\sqrt{b}} \by \dfrac{\sqrt{b}}{\sqrt{b}} \\[1em]
>    &= \dfrac{a \sqrt{b}}{b}.
> }
> $$

> __~lemma~.__ Iff ${a \gt 0,}$ ${b \gt 0,}$ and ${c \gt 0,}$
> 
> $$
>   \eqs{
>     \dfrac{a}{b + \sqrt{c}}
>     &= \dfrac{a}{b+\sqrt{c}}\by\dfrac{b-\sqrt{c}}{b-\sqrt{c}} \\[1em]
>     &= \dfrac{ab - a\sqrt{c}}{b^2 - c}.
>   }
> $$

</Grid>

## Logarithms
<Grid cols={2}>

> __~lemma~.__ ${\log_{b}\ar{m \by n} = \log_{b}(m) + \log_{b}(n).}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${\log_{b}\ar{\dfrac{m}{n}}=\log_{b}(m)-\log_{b}(n).}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${\log_{b}\ar{m^k}=k \log_{b}(m).}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${\log_{b}(1)=0.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${\log_{b}(b)=1.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${\log_{b}(b^k)=k.}$

> __~lemma~.__ ${b^{\log_{b}(k)}=k.}$

</Grid>
