# Introduction

This volume covers notes on probability. Single-handedly, probability is perhaps the one
area of mathematics most prone to abuse and misunderstanding. Worsening matters, it's
everywhere: disputing coin tosses, changing a public policy because of calculated lifespans, gender reveals, ensuring randomness, or attempting to game blackjack.
